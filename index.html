About Me

Hi! I'm Carlos, a Software Engineer.

I studied Physics (B.S.) and Mathematics (B.A.) at the University of Florida, and took some Computer Science courses as electives. After taking these courses I became interested in STEM Education, and in my final year at UF, I researched the Help-Seeking Behavior in Educational Video Games. In 2017, I started a PhD in Computer Science at Rutgers University. While there I researched Hierarchical Simulation of Social Media Networks and Multi-turn Open-Domain Conversational Agents. In 2020, I passed my qualifying exam, and exited the PhD program with a Master of Science in Computer Science.

With the M.S. and 3 years of research experience, I took a position as a Software Engineer at Systems & Technology Research that would allow me to get my bearings outside of Academia. After learning much and working with terrific scientists and engineers, I left that position to once again pursue my technical and professional interests in my current position as an Open Source Software Engineer for edX.

Interests
